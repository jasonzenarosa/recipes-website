Chicken Adobo

You already know what it is.

Ingredients

  • 10 Garlic Cloves (Finely chopped)
  • 2.5-3 lbs. Skinless Chicken Breast or Thighs (Cut into cubes)
  • 1/4 cup Soy Sauce
  • 1/4 cup Vinegar
  • 1 tsp. Black Peppercorns
  • 4 Bay Leaves
  • 4 tbsp. Corn Oil

Steps

  1. Combine all ingredients, mix well and marinate for 15-30 minutes.
  2. Cook on medium heat uncovered and do not mix for the first 5 minutes. Turn heat down to medium-low. Simmer covered for about 10 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  3. If there is still a lot of liquid after simmering, put the liquid in a separate bowl and set aside for later. If almost dry proceed to step 4.
  4. Uncover and turn up the heat to medium. Stir often until the liquid is gone and the chicken is golden brown and fragrant, about 3-5 minutes.
  5. Return the liquid that you have set aside, or if you don't have any, add about 1/2 cup of water. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  6. Serve with hardboiled egg and tomato!
