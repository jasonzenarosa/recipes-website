Beef Giniling

Filipino-style ground beef, eaten alongside rice, or for use in other recipes.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb. Lean Ground Beef
  • 1 Large Onion (Chopped)
  • 5 Garlic Cloves (Minced)
  • 1 Small Can of Tomato Sauce
  • 1 tbsp. Knorr Liquid Seasoning
  • 1 tbsp. Worcestershire Sauce
  • 1 tbsp. Soy Sauce
  • 1 tbsp. Black Pepper
  • Salt (to taste)
  • 1 Beef Boullion (Optional)

Steps

  1. In a large pan, saute garlic and onion until fragrant.
  2. Add ground beef and cook for 1 minute, stirring constantly.
  3. Add the tomato sauce, black pepper, knorr, and worcestershire sauce. Mix well.
  4. Simmer covered over medium low heat for 5 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  5. Simmer uncovered over medium high heat until liquid evaporates, stirring often.
  6. Serve or use in cobana, stuffed eggplant, potato balls, or spaghetti!
