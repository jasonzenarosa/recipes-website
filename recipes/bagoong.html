Cooked Bagoong

Shrimp paste!

Ingredients

  • 1/2 Onion (Finely Chopped)
  • 5 Garlic Cloves (Minced)
  • 5 Serrano Peppers
  • 1/4 cup Vinegar
  • 1 big jar of Pangasinan Bagoong
  • 1 small jar of Sweet Kamayan Bagoong

Steps

  1. Saute garlic and onion.
  2. Add both types of bagoong and the vinegar and mix well. Cook for 5 minutes.
  3. Add the serrano peppers.
  4. Simmer until the liquid is gone. The mixture should still be moist.
  5. Transfer to a jar and store in the fridge.
  6. Serve alongside kare-kare, fish, or barbecue!
