Kare Kare

Oxtail stew with a savory peanut sauce.

Ingredients

  • 2.5 lbs. Oxtail
  • 2.5 lbs. Tri-tip Roast
  • 2 tbsp. Oil
  • 1 Onion (Chopped)
  • 5 Garlic Cloves (Minced)
  • 8 oz. Tomato Sauce
  • 1 cup Creamy Peanut Butter
  • 1/4 cup Sugar
  • 1 Beef Boullion
  • Long Beans cut to 3 inch length
  • Bok Choy
  • Eggplant cut to 1 inch thickness

Steps

  1. Add tri-tip and oxtail to a pot, and add enough water to cover the meat.
  2. Turn heat up to high until the water comes to a boil, then reduce to medium-low and simmer for 1.5 to 2 hours, until tender but not soft.
  3. In a large pan, heat oil over medium heat and add onion, garlic, and tomato sauce. Saute for 2 minutes.
  4. Transfer the meat and half of the broth into the pan. Bring to a boil.
  5. Add peanut butter a little bit add a time, incorporating it fully into the broth.
  6. Add the sugar and the beef boullion cube.
  7. Simmer for another 5 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  8. Add broth or let boil until you achieve your desired consistency.
  9. Add salt and sugar to taste.
  10. Transfer stew to a large serving bowl.
  11. In the original pot, bring the leftover broth to a boil.
  12. Add the eggplant and long beans.
  13. Cook for 3 minutes, then add the bok choy.
  14. Cook for 1 minute, then transfer to a bowl.
  15. Serve!
