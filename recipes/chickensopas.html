Chicken Sopas

Like chicken noodle soup, but milkier (and Filipino). A good way to use leftover chicken.

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp. Vegetable Oil
  • 1 and 1/2 cup Cooked Chicken (Shredded)
  • 1 cup Cabbage (Shredded)
  • 1/2 cup Carrot (Julienned)
  • 3 Garlic Cloves (Crushed and Minced)
  • 1/2 Large Onion (Diced)
  • 8 oz. Uncooked Macaroni
  • 1 can of Evaporated Milk (12 oz.)
  • 1 Chicken Boullion
  • Salt or Patis (Fish Sauce) and Pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Put oil in a pot and turn heat to medium. Saute onion and garlic for about a minute, stirring frequently.
  2. Add chicken, salt or patis, pepper, and chicken boullion. Mix well.
  3. Add uncooked macaroni and 6 cups of water. Mix well.
  4. Stir frequently until the soup begins to boil. Then, reduce heat to medium-low and simmer for 10 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  5. Add the evaporated milk, cabbage and carrots and simmer for another 5 minutes, stirring occasionally. Add hot water if necessary.
  6. Serve!
