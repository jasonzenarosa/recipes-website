Mechado

Filipino-style beef stew.

Ingredients

  • 4 pcs. Boneless Beef Chuck Steak (Cut into 2 in. cubes)
  • 2 Onions (Diced)
  • 1/4 cup Soy Sauce
  • Juice of 1/2 of a Lemon
  • 2 tbsp. Black Peppercorns
  • 4 Bay Leaves
  • 1 small can of Tomato Sauce
  • 1 tbsp. Margarine
  • 1 Beef Boullion Cube

Steps

  1. Combine all ingredients in a pot.
  2. Cook on high heat until it comes to a boil, then reduce to medium-low and simmer for 1 hour or until tender, stirring occasionally.
  3. Serve alongside broiled potatoes, carrots, and bell peppers!
