Spaghetti

Mom's Filipino-style spaghetti.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb. Spaghetti
  • 4 tbsp. Butter
  • 1 lb. Beef Giniling
  • 1/2 lb. Filipino Hotdog (Sliced)
  • 1 can of Hunt's Meat Spaghetti Sauce
  • 1/4 cup Brown Sugar
  • 2/3 cup UFC Banana Ketchup

Steps

  1. Cook spaghetti according to package directions. Drain and add butter. Mix well.
  2. In a large pan over medium heat, add the Beef Giniling and stir.
  3. Let cook 2 minutes, then add the hot dog and stir.
  4. Let cook 3 minutes, then add spaghetti sauce, banana ketchup, and brown sugar. Mix well.
  5. Bring the mixture to a boil, then reduce the heat to medium-low and cover.
  6. Simmer for 15 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  7. Season with salt, sugar, and pepper to taste.
  8. Serve with shredded cheese!
